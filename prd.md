# [cite_start]Project Name: KSA-Project (B2B Supply Chain SaaS) 

## Vision
[cite_start]A platform connecting Merchants (Cafes/Restaurants) with Suppliers through an aggregated product catalog and AI-driven insights. [cite: 1, 2]

## Core Requirements
- [cite_start]**Language:** Fully English. [cite: 3]
- [cite_start]**Theme:** Support for Light & Dark modes. [cite: 3]
- [cite_start]**Primary Color:** #3D005E. [cite: 3]
- [cite_start]**Icons:** Phosphor Icons (Light). [cite: 3]
- [cite_start]**Responsive:** Mobile-ready web application. [cite: 3]

## Key Features for MVP
1. [cite_start]**Aggregated Product Cards:** Each product appears as a single card in the catalog, showing the lowest price and the total number of suppliers selling it. [cite: 56, 59]
2. [cite_start]**Merchant Dashboard:** High-impact visual dashboard with charts, stats (Total Orders, Expenses), and date filters. [cite: 45, 94]
3. [cite_start]**Product Detail Page:** 3-column layout showing product info, variant selection, and a list of all suppliers selling that product with their prices. [cite: 73, 76]
4. [cite_start]**Checkout Flow:** Cart summary, fake payment step (Credit Card/Apple Pay), and a success popup with custom colored confetti (#3D005E, #FFD680). [cite: 82, 88, 37, 90]
5. [cite_start]**Supplier Add Product:** Catalog search functionality (Name/Barcode) to find and link products to the supplier's inventory. [cite: 137, 138]
6. [cite_start]**Simulated AI Chat:** A sidebar chat (Co-pilot style) with pre-defined questions and delayed dummy responses to simulate AI thinking. [cite: 191, 194, 196]
